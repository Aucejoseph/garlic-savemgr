## 🔍 Using garlic-savemgr

After installation, open garlic-savemgr. You will see a simple window that guides you through managing your save files.

### Main Features:

- **Backup Saves:** Connect your PS5 or storage device and select save files to copy to your PC.
- **Restore Saves:** Move saved files from your PC back to your console or USB.
- **Organize Saves:** Rename, delete, or move save files within the app.
- **Automatic Detection:** The app detects save files from your connected devices automatically.

### Step-by-step to back up your save files:

1. Connect your PS5 or USB storage to your PC.
2. Open garlic-savemgr.
3. Click the **Backup** tab.
4. Select the save files you want to copy.
5. Choose a folder on your PC to save them.
6. Click **Start Backup**.
7. Wait for the process to finish. Your saves are now stored safely on your PC.

### Step-by-step to restore save files back to your PS5:

1. Connect your PS5 or USB to your PC.
2. Open garlic-savemgr.
3. Click the **Restore** tab.
4. Select the save files on your PC you want to transfer.
5. Choose the target device (PS5 or USB).
6. Click **Start Restore**.
7. Wait for the app to copy the files back to your device.
